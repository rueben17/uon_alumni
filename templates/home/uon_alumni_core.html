Foundations of UoNAA

The strategic plan sets UoNAA priorities for the next five years towards the vision. This strategic plan is purposefully built from the following tenets:

Motto.

Our Alumni Our Strength!

Vision.

To be an influential Alumni Association that is engaged and driven by a shared commitment to excellence and services.

Mission.

To empower, connect, and engage the alumni of the University of Nairobi, fostering a culture of excellence, service and innovation.
We are committed to mobilizing resources, building strategic partnerships and networks, increasing memberships and strengthening student- alumni relations.

Core Values

UoNAA has core values that reflect the principles and ideals that guide her as she works towards achieving her vision and mission as outlined in the strategic plan.

These include: Integrity, accountability, transparency, loyalty, professionalism, excellence, care, innovation, service, Legacy, and environmental stewardship.

Integrity and Professionalism

Integrity is the foundation of our Association. We conduct ourselves with honesty, transparency, and ethical conduct in all interactions, ensuring the trust and credibility of the UONAA.

Collaboration

We recognize the power of collaboration. We build strong partnerships and networks with alumni, educational institutions, government agencies, and organizations to achieve our shared goals and advance the interests of our members.

Environmental Stewardship

We are committed to responsible environmental management. We value sustainability and actively promote eco-friendly practices in all our activities, events, and operations, contributing to a greener and more sustainable future.

Excellence

We are committed to upholding the highest standards of excellence in all our activities, programs, and services, reflecting the prestige of our alma mater, the University of Nairobi.

Inclusivity

We celebrate the rich diversity of our alumni community and are dedicated to fostering an inclusive environment where every alumnus, regardless of background or affiliation, feels valued and welcomed.

Innovation

We embrace innovation and creativity to adapt to evolving needs and challenges. We seek innovative solutions to enhance alumni engagement and contribute positively to society.

Legacy

We honor the rich heritage and traditions of the University of Nairobi, preserving its legacy for future generations of alumni.

Lifelong Learning

We promote continuous learning and personal development among our alumni. We provide opportunities for skill enhancement, knowledge sharing, and career advancement.

Respect

We treat all individuals with respect, dignity, and courtesy. We value diverse perspectives and encourage open dialogue within our alumni community.

Service

Our alumni are dedicated to giving back to our university and the broader community. We are committed to volunteerism, community service, and supporting initiatives that create a positive impact.
