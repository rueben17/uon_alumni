Foundations of UoNAA

The strategic plan sets UoNAA priorities for the next five years towards the vision. This strategic plan is purposefully built from the following tenets:

Motto.

Our Alumni Our Strength!

Vision.

To be an influential Alumni Association that is engaged and driven by a shared commitment to excellence and services.

Mission.

To empower, connect, and engage the alumni of the University of Nairobi, fostering a culture of excellence, service and innovation.
We are committed to mobilizing resources, building strategic partnerships and networks, increasing memberships and strengthening student- alumni relations.
